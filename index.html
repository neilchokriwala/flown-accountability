🚀 Quick Setup - JSONBin Database

Step 1: Create Your Free JSONBin Account

1. Go to jsonbin.io
2. Click "Sign Up" (free account)
3. Verify your email

Step 2: Create a Bin

1. Click "Create a Bin"
2. Name it: "Flown Accountability"
3. Set it to Public (for reading)
4. Paste this initial data:

  "links": {}
}

5. Click "Create Bin"
